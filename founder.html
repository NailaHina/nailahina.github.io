Engr Dr Naila Hina is a visionary poet, multilingual author, and strategist, blending tradition with innovation across Urdu, Persian, Spanish, and English. She is a former engineering university instructor, international award-winning author of over 860 books, and a cultural thought leader from Karachi, Pakistan.

Professional Journey & Qualifications

Dr Hina holds a B.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering from NED University of Engineering & Technology, Karachi, along with MBA and CMA degrees, as well as multiple honorary doctorates. She has served as Continental Manager of Asia at the World Press Agency, editor, translator, science journalist, and educator.

Literary & Cultural Contributions

She has authored a wide range of works including Bahisht e Char Saat, Shadows of the Sun, Cosmic Dreams, Labor of Love, Hayat e Javedan, Fragrance of a Cave Girl, and more. Dr Hina pioneered the Gammo style of poetry and was the first to translate Imru al Qays' poetry into Urdu. Her literary universe intertwines myth, romance, politics, and cosmic symbolism.

Scientific & Humanitarian Focus

Dr Hina emphasizes critical thinking, scientific awareness, and the promotion of peace, education, and cultural exchange. She envisions a united world through multilingual literature and poetry without borders.

Recognition & Awards

She has received numerous awards and accolades for literature, peace, and humanitarian contributions, and her poems have been published and translated internationally. Certificates, honors, and notable achievements are available on the Recognition & Awards page.

Engr. Dr. Naila Hina

Visionary Poet · Multilingual Author · Cultural Strategist

About

Engr. Dr. Naila Hina is a visionary poet and strategist, blending tradition with innovation across Urdu, Persian, Spanish, and English. Her work honors cultural legacy while embracing mythic, romantic, and political symbolism.

An international award-winning author of more than 860 multilingual books, she is widely recognized for her contribution to global literature, translation, and cultural thought.

Literary Domains

Poetry

Mythic, romantic, political, and metaphysical verse across civilizations.

Fiction

Epic narratives exploring destiny, memory, and symbolism.

Translation & Editing

Bridging languages, cultures, and literary traditions.

Strategy & Thought

Literature as vision, resistance, and intellectual architecture.

About

Engr. Dr. Naila Hina is a visionary poet and strategist, blending tradition with innovation across Urdu, Persian, Spanish, and English. Her work honors cultural legacy while embracing mythic, romantic, and political symbolism.

A former Engineering University Instructor and an international award-winning author of more than 860 multilingual books, she is a poet from Karachi, Pakistan, celebrated as Best Writer of the Decade, editor, and translator. She holds the honorary title of Literary Colonel at Story Mirror and has been recommended for the Nobel Prize for Literature (2022, 2023).

Her literary universe often unfolds as both an interstellar adventure and an introspective exploration of light, darkness, memory, and destiny.
